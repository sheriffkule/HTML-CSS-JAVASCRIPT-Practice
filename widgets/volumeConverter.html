    About Volume Converter Pro

    This app allows you to convert between different volume units quickly and accurately.

    Common Value Conversions

    • 1 teaspoon = 5 milliliters
    • 1 tablespoon = 15 milliliters
    • 1 fluid ounce = 30 milliliters
    • 1 cup = 240 milliliters
    • 1 pint = 480 milliliters
    • 1 quart = 960 milliliters
    • 1 gallon = 3.8 liters
    • 1 cubic meter = 1000 liters

    How to Use

    1. Enter a value in the "Form" field.
    2. Select the unit you're converting from.
    3. Select the unit you want to convert to.
    4. The result will appear automatically.
    5. Use the swap button to reverse the units.
    6. Save your favorite conversions for quick access.
