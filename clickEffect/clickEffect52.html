FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of our website, fill in your details including name, email, phone, and password, and you'll be ready to go. We'll send you a confirmation email to verify your account.

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, please check your spam folder or request a new verification email from your account dashboard.

Can i cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. You can cancel directly from your account settings.

After cancellation, you'll receive a confirmation email and can reactivate your subscription at any time by logging back into your account.

how do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on "Forgot Password" on login page. Enter email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link will expire in 24 hours for security reasons.

For enhanced security, we recommend using a password manager to generate and store strong, unique passwords for all your online accounts.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! We have iOS and Android apps available for download in their respective app stores. Our mobile app offers all the functionality of our web platform with additional features like biometric login and offline access to your documents.

The App is optimize for both phones and tablets, with automatic sync across all your devices. Push notifications keep you updated on important activities.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7. you can reach us via live chat at any time.

For faster service, have your account information ready when contacting support.
