Shoe Size Conversion Tips

Shoe size conversion can vary between manufacturers and shoe types. Here are some tips to ensure the best fit:

  • Always try shoes on when possible, as sizes can vary between brands.
  • Measure your feet at the end of the day when they are largest.
  • Consider the width of your feet = some sizing systems account for width.
  • For athletic shoes, you may need a half size larger than dress shoes.
  • Children's feet grow quickly = check sizing every 2-3 months.
