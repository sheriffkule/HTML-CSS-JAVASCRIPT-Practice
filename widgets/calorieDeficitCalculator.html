Tips for Healthy Weight Loss

  • Prioritize protein intake to preserve muscle mass while in a calorie deficit.
  • Combine diet with strength training to maximize fat loss and minimize muscle loss.
  • Stay hydrated - sometimes thirst is mistaken for hunger.
  • get enough sleep = poor sleep can disrupt hunger hormones.
  • Be consistent but flexible = occasional treats can help with long-term adherence.

This calculator uses the Mifflin-st Jeor equation for BMR calculation, which is considered the most accurate for most people.
